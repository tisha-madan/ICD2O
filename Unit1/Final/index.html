LONDON

Welcome to London! Prepare for a huge, culturally diverse city brimming with history, iconic landmarks like the Tower of London and Buckingham Palace, and world-class museums. To make the most of your visit book attractions in advance, and pack comfortable shoes and an umbrella for the variable weather. Embrace the local customs and explore bustling markets and beautiful parks.
